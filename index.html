Our Heritage

Where Royal Kitchens Come Alive

✦

Daawat-e-Nizam carries forward the 400-year-old culinary legacy of the Nizam's royal kitchens — now based in Altrincham, proudly serving Greater Manchester. Every dish honours that tradition through recipes passed through generations and ingredients sourced purely for quality.

  • 01
    Asli Desi Ghee — Always
    No shortcuts. Every biryani, every curry, every haleem enriched with pure desi ghee — the way it was always meant to be made.
  • 02
    Heirloom Masala Blends
    Whole spices hand-ground fresh for every single order. No pre-mixes, no packets — only the real thing.
  • 03
    Dum Pukht Technique
    Sealed and slow-cooked using the ancient dum method — trapping every fragrance, every flavour inside the pot.
