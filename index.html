Our Heritage

Where Royal Kitchens
Come Alive

Daawat-e-Nizam carries forward the 400-year-old culinary legacy of Hyderabad's Nizam royal kitchens — now based in Altrincham, proudly serving Greater Manchester. Every dish honours that tradition through recipes passed down through generations and ingredients sourced purely for quality.

🧈

Asli Desi Ghee — Always

No shortcuts. Every biryani, every curry, every haleem enriched with pure desi ghee — the way it was always meant to be.

🌿

Heirloom Masala Blends

Whole spices hand-ground fresh for every order. Authentic Hyderabadi blends — never a shortcut powder.

🔥

Dum Pukht Technique

Sealed pots, slow heat — the ancient dum method locks in aroma and creates that signature flavour no other technique can replicate.
